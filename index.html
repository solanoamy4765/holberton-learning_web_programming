Corgi's are dogs loved by many.

Corgi's go back to the 10th century and are believed to be used as herding dogs. They are good with other animals, especially other dogs. Corgi's are very smart and love attention. They are very energrtic and enjoy playing. They are loyal to their owners and will never leave your side. These dogs will follow you around all day.

Corgi's are amazing pets!
